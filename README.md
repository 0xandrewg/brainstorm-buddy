Brainstorm Buddy: Stress-testing Your Ideas
A Claude skill that challenges your ideas before you commit to them. Instead of agreeing with you and moving on to the tasks, it finds blind spots, pokes holes, and pressure-tests your thinking so you walk away with stronger ideas.
What It Does
When you share an idea, this skill:

Mirrors it back — restates your idea to confirm understanding (and expose fuzzy thinking)
Identifies blind spots — surfaces what you haven't considered: hidden assumptions, opportunity costs, who loses, what breaks at scale
Steel-mans the strongest objection — gives you the hardest version of the pushback you'd hear from an investor, competitor, or skeptical customer
Leaves you with a harder question — one pointed question you should be able to answer confidently before moving forward

Install
Download brainstorm-challenger.skill and add it to your Claude skills.
When It Triggers

Brainstorming or pitching ideas
Asking "what do you think of this plan/approach?"
Saying "challenge me," "poke holes," "what am I missing"
Floating any business idea, product concept, or strategic decision

Philosophy
Challenge first, validate second. The goal is better ideas.
